Tomb RaidAARRR

Game created in a team of 4 during Brackeys Game Jam 2024.2

During the Brackeys Game Jam 2024.2, the theme was "the calm before the storm". Our team of 4 decided to create a character who steals treasures in a room until they find a cursed treasure. Once the cursed treasure is discovered, the walls start closing in, and traps are triggered.

I was the sole developer on the project, which made development challenging, as I could only work in the evenings. The rest of the team handled other aspects of the game. Thanks to their talent, they were able to save the project and create a playable and fun game.

The game is playable directly in the browser and is available on Itch.io. You can check it out here: Tomb RaidAARRR on Itch.io
