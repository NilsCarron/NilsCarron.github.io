El ChupaCabra

A VR game created in class in a team of 3

This video game allows the player to put on virtual gloves, train with three sandbags of different weights, and step into the ring to fight El Chupacabra.
The fighter strikes at random intervals, giving the player an opportunity to counterattack. This continues until the massive El Chupacabra is defeated.

This game was my first venture into VR development. It was challenging, as we had limited resources (one VR headset and some old computers).
Overall, it was a very enjoyable project to develop, and I'm pleased with how the game feels.

We used Unreal Engine 4 exclusively and were impressed by how comprehensive the software was, with many useful VR tools.
We mainly used Blueprints, as the game didn't require many scripts running simultaneously, making development easier.
