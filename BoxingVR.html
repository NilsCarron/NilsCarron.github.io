El ChupaCabra

A VR game created in class in team of 3

This video Game gives the player the ability to grab his gloves, train on 3 bags of sands of differents weights, and get on the ring and fight El Chupacabra
The fighter will hit at random moments, wich will give the player the opportunity to retaliate. This goes on and on until the gigantic El Chupacabra crumbles.

This game was my first developpement in VR, it was kind of hard, since we did'nt had a lot of ressources (1VR helmet, and some old computers).
Overall, this was a very fun project to develop, and I'm very happy with the feeling of the game.

We used entierly the Unreal Engine, and we were surprised how complete was the software, with a lot of usefull tools on VR.
We mainly used blueprints, since the game did'nt had a lot of scripts runnings at the same time, and it was easyer to develop.
