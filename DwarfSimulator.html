Dwarf Simulator

AI Project Created with Unity

In this project, a colony of dwarfs interacts dynamically, with the dwarfs dodging and following each other (as boids do). Once a group of dwarfs is formed, they head to the mine. When a dwarf finds a nugget, he returns to the tavern. If a dwarf drinks too much, he vomits, causing the other dwarfs to run away.

For this project, I was working solo and aimed to include at least two animations to bring the dwarfs to life. The dwarf sprite was sourced from an old game with free-to-use assets. I used Mixamo to rig the character and add two animations.

This was less work compared to when I animated the characters in "Sweat Steam & Struggle," thanks to the auto-rigging tools. Nonetheless, it was fascinating to rig a character from scratch.
