Meme Lords

App created in a team of 3 during the Android Studio class at IUT

During the Android Studio class, our team decided to create a mobile app for meme creation. Most meme generator apps add a watermark to the final image unless the user pays. Meme Lords creates the image without a watermark for free.

Moreover, users can log in with Reddit to access the most popular templates for their memes. Developing this app was very fun; we wanted to add an AI feature that could create memes automatically. However, we didn't add this functionality as it would have made the app too heavy.
