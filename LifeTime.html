Life Time

Game created in teams of 8 during a semester

The video game workshop class led us to the creation of a game with the theme "Your life is currency". The game has a Third person view the main character is a wizzard. The player must choose how to spend his time, which will cost him life, the healthbar will decrement over time once in the level. The player have a certain amount of time that he'll spend on the level to make the platforms least for example.

In a team of 8 our objective is to create a completed game fun and playable.
