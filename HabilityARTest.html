HabilityARDemo

Technical test developed in 5 days

In this demo, the user needs to scan the company's logo to make a monkey and a maze appear in augmented reality.

Once the logo is properly detected, the player must guide the monkey to collect all the bananas scattered throughout the scene. This was my second time using AR technology, and the Vuforia API greatly aided in the development process.

The project was quite interesting to develop, and I rediscovered some of the challenges I encountered with WARgames, particularly with scale and image clipping. However, Vuforia significantly mitigated the clipping issue. Additionally, since Vuforia can scan any image, the potential applications for this technology are vast.
