Python Software for H'ability

Custom database management tool

During my time at H'ability, I was approached by the sales department to develop a managing tool for their contact database. I designed and developed the application independently, from concept to final delivery, including comprehensive documentation for its use and future updates.

I was tasked with creating two versions of the application, each addressing the problem from a different perspective. Both versions aimed to generate a CSV document that could be utilized by the company's sales team. I wrote the entire application in Python, focusing on making it as user-friendly as possible for individuals without software development experience.
