Schoolmap

Site created during my 2nd year of IUT

During my second year of DUT, I developed a site in PHP as part of a team of three.

This site was designed to display the list of available schools around a given location on a map. We used Leaflet, a JavaScript library, to display and manipulate the map.

The main task was the extraction, storage, and utilization of data about these schools. To achieve this, we retrieved the data via an external API, stored it in JSON, and reused it through AJAX functions.
